Our Story

Hola, my name is Wendy. I'm 29 years old going on 30 (sadness). So I was having a late midlife crisis working as an analyst to help big companies can lower costs using old software. Took my first international trip to Tokyo (Sorry Canada you don't count).

And fell in love with their crepes! That is when I knew deep in my soul I was to be a crepe maker. So here I am trying to make in the Big Peach State with my Tokyo Crepes.
